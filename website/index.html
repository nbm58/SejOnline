Serpent Studios



Serpent Studios is a team of students from Northern Arizona University working on the game Sej Online for Instructor Patrick Kelley. Sej is a fictional game that was used as a plot point in the novel Serpent's Reach by author C. J. Cherryh. At the end of the novel, the author provided the rules for the game, which fans found simple but interesting to play.
